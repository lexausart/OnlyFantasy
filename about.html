Tian Peng

Discord: Saltamiya#5905

Tian Peng is a tamawo, a Filipino creature similar to a Moogle and fairy.

Tamawo are very territorial and express their feelings by throwing objects such as dirt, flowers, or stones.
Of course, she loves her family! Fortunata sends motiviational packages and postcards to Da Qi and Tian Peng while she's out researching.

A tamawo is made when another tamawo kidnaps a child and feeds a child their food. It is up to interpretation on the legitimacy of her family life.

Her best friend is Sunny and she teases her by shape-shifting into Sunny's wife, Blaire.

Fortunata Peng

Tuna, for short

Discord: Saltamiya#5905
Twitter/FB/Instagram: @Saltamiya

Fortunata Peng is an aswang, a Filipino creature akin to a shape-shifting vampire.

Aswang differ based on ethnolinguistic groups in the Philippines but Fortunata has a long, red, thread-like tongue she keeps hidden by speaking with few words.
She takes the form of what she wants at will. At the moment she makes sure to look attractive to her wife, Tian.

An aswang from her specific ethnolinguistic group also displays characteristics of a manananggal, in which she can separate her torso with entrails hanging out from the bottom half of her body and fly around.

As Da Qi doesn't have a father figure in his life, she dons a moustache to assert masculinity. Eventually she hopes to fill the void of other family figures as well, such as sister, brother, aunt, uncle, cousin, and so forth.

Her favorite food is corpses with her favorite drink being blood. As a father figure to Da, she has shed this aspect of her life to the best of her ability and enables him to live a completely "normal" life.
